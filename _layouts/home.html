Announcing the Science of Blockchain Conference (SBC) 2025, co-organized by IC3, Stanford Center for Blockchain Research, and UC Berkeley RDI.

The conference focuses on technical innovations in the blockchain ecosystem, bringing together researchers and practitioners working in the space.

When: August 3-7, 2025
Where: Martin Luther King Jr. Building, UC Berkeley & David Brower Center, Berkeley
Open to: Researchers, engineers, and industry leaders
Visit: SBC'25 Official Page

CONFERENCE

Please visit the SBC'25 official page for details on call for papers, registration, accommodations, affiliated workshops, and more.
For inquiries email ic3.sbc25@rdi.berkeley.edu.

SPONSORSHIP

If you are interested in sponsoring this year's edition, please find more information here along with the sponsorship interest form and details. For other requests, please feel free to reach out to the Berkeley RDI team at rdi_events@berkeley.edu, and we will get back to you soon.

AFFILIATED WORKSHOPS

Workshops will be hosted on August 3 and August 7, 2025, at UC Berkeley and the nearby David Brower Center.

  • StarkWare Scholar Summit #2
  • Blockchain Application Stanford Summit (BASS) at SBC 2025
  • SBC DAO Workshop 2025 (Metagov & Decentralization Research Center)
  • Impact Summit (Metagov)
  • Science and Engineering of Consensus (Tse Lab Stanford)
  • SMEV-SBC - Flashbots

Looking forward to seeing you there!
