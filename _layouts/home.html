Announcing the Science of Blockchain Conference (SBC) 2025, co-organized by IC3, Stanford Center for Blockchain Research, and UC Berkeley RDI.

The conference focuses on technical innovations in the blockchain ecosystem, bringing together researchers and practitioners working in the space.

📅 When: August 3-7, 2025
📍 Where: Martin Luther King Jr. Building, UC Berkeley & David Brower Center, Berkeley
🎓 Open to: Researchers, engineers, and industry leaders
🔗 Visit: SBC'25 Official Page
